Our story and mission

Our Story

The Midnight Echoes was formed in 2018 in Charlotte, North Carolina. We are a group of musicians dedicated to preserving and modernizing traditional music for contemporary audiences.

Our music bridges traditional rhythms and melodies with modern instrumentation and production. We believe in the power of music to connect generations and cultures.

Through our songs, we tell stories of heritage, love, and the immigrant experience.
