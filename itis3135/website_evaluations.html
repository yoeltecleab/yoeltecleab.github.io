Website Evaluations

arngren.net

Arngren is an infamous example of poor web design, featuring a chaotic layout, excessive use of colors, and a lack of visual hierarchy. The site attempts to sell a wide variety of products but overwhelms visitors with clutter and confusion.

  • Contrast: The site uses many clashing colors and backgrounds, making it hard to focus on any one area. There is little effective contrast to guide the eye.
  • Repetition: There is no consistent style or repeated elements; every section looks different, which adds to the confusion.
  • Alignment: Items are scattered all over the page with no clear alignment. Text and images are placed haphazardly, making the site look messy and unprofessional.
  • Proximity: Related items are not grouped together. Products, prices, and descriptions are all jumbled, making it difficult to understand what belongs together.

jetbrains.com

jetbrains.com is a well-designed site for a software company, offering a clean, modern interface with clear navigation and strong visual hierarchy. The site effectively communicates its products and services to visitors.

  • Contrast: The site uses color and whitespace effectively to highlight important elements and create a clear visual hierarchy.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of fonts, colors, and button styles throughout the site reinforces branding and usability.
  • Alignment: All elements are neatly aligned, creating a professional and organized appearance that is easy to scan.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped together logically, making it easy for users to find information and understand relationships between elements.
